Therapy Programs

At Sunrise, we strive to foster a welcoming sense of community, to challenge each participant to new levels of personal achievement, and to maintain the highest possible standards of service with respect to safety and industry best practices. All lessons are taught by CanTRA certified instructors, with up to 3 trained volunteers assisting each rider, depending on the rider's needs. Each lesson is tailored to the rider's individual therapy goals, interests and abilities.

We are located on a beautiful 102‑acre farm in Puslinch, Ontario, between Guelph and Cambridge, and only minutes from Highway 401. In addition to therapeutic riding lessons, which are taught by Canadian Therapeutic Riding Association (CanTRA) certified instructors, Sunrise offers inclusive programs open to individuals with and without special needs.
