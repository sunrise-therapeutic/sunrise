Group Visits

Therapeutic riding lessons are available for classes of children with special needs. The "School Group" program generally consists of a set of four visits to the farm (held on subsequent weeks) for a group riding lesson. Prior to the first ride, a Sunrise Instructor will visit the classroom, or a site visit may be arranged for the class.

Other agency groups are welcome to book a program at Sunrise. Non-riding options are available, including grooming or driving. Please contact Sunrise's Program Coordinator to discuss your goals, suitable program options and booking availability.
