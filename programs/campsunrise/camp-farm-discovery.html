Camp 2025 Registration is Now Open!!

Farm Discovery Camp is a farm themed camp for children 5-8 years of age. Our camp provides one week session options with the opportunity to have hands-on horse experience.

Farm Discovery Camp activities include:

  • • Grooming & Leading Miniature Horses
  • • Archery
  • • Swimming
  • • Low Ropes Challenge Course
  • • Accessible Playground
  • • Frog Pond
  • • Arts & Crafts
  • • Nature Programs
  • • Outdoor Games

Fridays are theme days at Sunrise! We encourage campers to dress up and show their camp spirit according to the theme!

New campers with a special need or diagnosis are required to come to Sunrise for a pre-camp assessment with the Camp Coordinator. A Physician Referral Form and Individual Profile Form is also required. Registration is complete when all paperwork and payment has been received and assessment is completed.
