Embracing inclusion. Unleashing potential.

Sunrise is proud to offer inclusive day camps for children and youth aged 5 – 13+. We provide structured programming and theme days throughout the summer. One of our camp sessions has an Interdenominational Christian theme. Our welcoming family atmosphere places an emphasis on teamwork and respect for others.

Sunrise provides a safe and friendly atmosphere along with:

  • Ontario Camp Association accreditation
  • Canadian Therapeutic Riding Association certified instructors
  • Certified lifeguards
  • Low ratio of counsellors to children (1:6 ratio plus each group will have a Leader-in-Training)
  • Mature, experienced staff with First aid and CPR training
