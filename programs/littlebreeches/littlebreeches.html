Little Breeches is an inclusive farm-based program for children aged 5-8 and 9-12 years old.

This program provides the opportunity for children to visit the Sunrise farm to learn about horses and participate in horse care and other activities such as:

  • Weekly seasonal and horse-related themes
  • Interactive and hands-on activities
  • Grooming, leading & washing miniature horses
  • Equine Assisted Learning (EAL)
  • Crafts
  • Outdoor Games
  • Playground
  • Archery
  • Low Ropes Course

New participants with a diagnosis/special need are required to come to Sunrise before the start of the program for an assessment with the Little Breeches Coordinator. The assessment allows for the Little Breeches Coordinator to meet the participant and determine if any additional support will be needed for a successful experience.

Participants with a diagnosis/special need are required to complete the Physician Referral and Individual Profile Form, which can be found below under the Forms heading.
