Little Breeches Program

About the Program

Little Breeches is an inclusive farm-based program for children aged 5-8 and 9-12 years old. The program provides the opportunity for children to visit the Sunrise farm to learn about horses and participate in horse care and other activities such as:

  • Weekly seasonal and horse-related themes
  • Interactive and hands-on activities
  • Grooming, leading & washing miniature horses
  • Equine Assisted Learning (EAL)
  • Crafts
  • Outdoor Games
  • Playground
  • Archery
  • Low Ropes Course

Admission Procedures

New participants with a diagnosis/special need are required to complete the Physician Referral (PDF) and Introduction Letter & Individual Profile (PDF), and to come to Sunrise before the start of the program for an assessment with the Little Breeches Coordinator. The assessment allows for the Little Breeches Coordinator to meet the participant and determine if any additional support will be needed for a successful experience.

Forms and Waiver

Please complete the following forms:

  • Little Breeches & Mini Moments Application Form (ages 5-8) or the Little Breeches & Mini Moments Application Form (ages 9-12)
  • Physician Referral (PDF)
  • Introduction Letter & Individual Profile (PDF)
  • Bodily Contact Policy Acknowledgment (R10), Photo Release & Liability Waiver (DOCX) and attach it to the application form.
