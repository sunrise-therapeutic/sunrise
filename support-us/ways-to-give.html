One-time or Monthly Donations

One-time or ongoing monthly donations to help us cover general operating and other costs are deeply appreciated. Your donation will be used to fund our general operations (such as utilities, feed, bedding, veterinary care, etc.) by addressing specific needs as identified by Sunrise staff.

Sunrise accepts one-time or ongoing monthly monetary donations of any amount. All donations of $20 or more will receive a tax receipt.

To make a donation, please click the Donate Now button above, and select the amount and type of donation (one-time or monthly), or call us at (519) 837-0558 x 31 to make a donation over the phone.

In-kind Donations of Financial Securities

Gifts of publicly traded securities—including stocks, mutual funds, and ETFs—offer one of the most tax-efficient ways to support Sunrise’s programs.

When donations are made directly to Sunrise, capital gains tax is eliminated so more of your gift goes to supporting Sunrise. Giving to Sunrise in-kind can be an effective part of your year-end financial planning.

Donate a Car

Sunrise is partnering with Donate a Car Canada to accept your car donation! You will be provided free towing, or you can drop off your vehicle to maximize your donation. When you donate your car, truck, RV, boat, or motorcycle to SUNRISE through Donate A Car Canada, it will either be recycled or re-sold (depending on its condition, age and location). Donate a Car Canada will look after all the details to make it easy for Sunrise to benefit. You will receive a tax receipt after your car donation has been processed!

Supporting the Sunrise Therapy Forest

Adopt-a-Tree (Annual)

Support the Sunrise Therapy Forest by 'adopting' one of several designated trees for a one-year period. Donors receive a card with information about their adopted tree. There are multiple sponsors per tree at this level. A recognition/sign unveiling event will be held every September. All those who have supported the forest project in the past year will receive an invitation! All supporters receive a tax receipt and certificate, suitable for giving as a gift to someone being recognized.

For more information about the Forest and how to make a donation, please visit the Sunrise Therapy Forest page.
