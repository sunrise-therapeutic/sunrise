Drop it off for Sunrise


What can be dropped off?

Any of the following can be dropped off at Sunrise – your donations will be used to help support Sunrise programs.

  • Canadian Tire Money
  • Metal Recycling – (in black bin in parking lot) Stainless steel, aluminum, copper, tin, etc.
  • Tack – We can accept used tack and related supplies that are in very good/ like new condition. If you are donating riding or horse equipment, please note tax receipts will reflect the current market value of the item accepted for the program. It should be understood that we sell items on consignment that we are unable to use for the program. The proceeds will be used to purchase adaptive/program equipment. Please note that we cannot accept used helmets. Please call ahead to ensure someone can meet you to accept delivery. We request a Tack Donation Form be submitted with your donation.
  • Prizes / Auction Items – We are always seeking gifts-in-kind of NEW, quality items for upcoming events. Examples include horse, culinary or garden themed giftware.
