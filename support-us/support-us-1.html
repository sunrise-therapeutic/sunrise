Support Us!

Your generous support helps us continue to provide life-changing programs for individuals with special needs.

You can make one-time or monthly donations to help us with general operating costs, or support a specific fund or program that resonates with you. We also accept donations of items on our wish list.
Below are some of the ways you can support what we do at Sunrise.

One-time or Monthly Donations

One-time or ongoing monthly donations to help us cover general operating and other costs are deeply appreciated. Your donation will be used to fund our general operations (such as utilities, feed, bedding, veterinary care, etc.) by addressing specific needs as identified by Sunrise staff.
