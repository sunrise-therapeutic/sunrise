Celebrate Sunrise!

Our "Celebrate Sunrise!" is a cornerstone fundraising event for our year-round therapy programs.

Join us Sunday June 22nd, 2025 and help make a difference in the lives of children and adults with disabilities!

  • Enjoy delicious food prepared by local chefs (cash bar available)
  • Participate in our silent auction, with a wide selection of great prizes
  • Enjoy our exciting live auction- led by outstanding auctioneer Robert Milburn
  • Meet special guests
  • Learn more about Sunrise programs, and find out why Sunrise is a Canadian leader in the field of therapeutic riding!
