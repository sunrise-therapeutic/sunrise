Celebrate Sunrise!

"Celebrate Sunrise!" is our annual cornerstone fundraising event for our year-round therapy programs, traditionally held each spring.

The 2025 event was held on Sunday June 22nd, 2025 and helped make a difference in the lives of children and adults with disabilities! This year's celebration was a great success, as participants:

  • Enjoyed delicious food prepared by local chefs (cash bar available)
  • Participated in our silent auction, with a wide selection of great prizes
  • Enjoyed our exciting live auction- led by outstanding auctioneer Robert Milburn
  • Met special guests
  • Learned more about Sunrise programs, and find out why Sunrise is a Canadian leader in the field of therapeutic riding!
