Description
Fantasy is a grey Arab pony mare, with a great disposition, who enjoys her role as a therapeutic riding pony. Being grey, it is not easy to keep her clean. She is also quite fond of the mud, and it is not uncommon to see her covered head to toe, much to the chagrin of anyone who has to get her ready for her lesson!
