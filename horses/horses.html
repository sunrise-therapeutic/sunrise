Our Barn Cats

In addition to our wonderful horses and miniature horses, Sunrise is also home to two adorable barn cats. Molly and her little sister Dora arrived at Sunrise in September of 2018 just 16 weeks old. For the first month and a half they were at Sunrise they lived in the office and kept the staff more than just a little amused with their antics. These feline friends help keep the barn free of unwanted pests while entertaining everyone at Sunrise with their playful antics and affectionate nature.
