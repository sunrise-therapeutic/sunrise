Pevensie

Born: 1999

Gender: Mare

Breed: Norwegian Fjord

Height: 15.1 hh

Colour: Dun

Peven, as she is known around the farm, is a Norwegian Fjord mare, who is extremely dependable and trustworthy.

Pevensie worked in a Narnia focussed summer camp in British Columbia for many years (hence her name from the characters in the book by C.S. Lewis) Peven then lived for a while on a private farm in Ontario before coming to Sunrise.

Her Ontario owners kindly donated her, wanting her to be enjoyed by the children in the Sunrise therapeutic riding programs. She has certainly become an integral part of the team.

Sponsor: A Friend of Sunrise
