Rolex

Born: 1999

Gender: Gelding

Breed: Appaloosa/Thoroughbred

Height: 15.3 hh

Colour: Blanket Appaloosa

Rolex is an Appaloosa/Thoroughbred gelding, and is as well known for his trustworthy and calm personality as he is for his beautiful blanket Appaloosa colouring. He is one the longest serving and most valued members our therapy horse team.
