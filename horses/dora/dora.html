Description
Dora is our little explorer. Ever since she arrived at Sunrise she has kept staff, participants and visitors extremely amused. While Dora was living in the office her greatest curiosity was the photo copier. This magical device that spit out paper was a mystery that Dora took upon herself to try and solve. Many hours were spent investigating, watching and sneaking up on this enigmatic device with the belief that she would eventually figure it out. Now that Dora and her sister live outside in the barn there are whole new mysteries to solve, amazing places to explore and the most fantastic new things to play with.
