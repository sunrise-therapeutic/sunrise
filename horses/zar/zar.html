Zar

Born: 2015

Gender: Gelding

Breed: Canadian

Height: 15.3 hh

Colour: Black

Zar is a solid black purebred Canadian Horse. This breed was officially made Canada's national breed in 2002 by an Act of Parliament.
Zar arrived at Sunrise in the Spring of 2019. He has a great temperament and calm demeanor. Zar is a member of our younger team of horses, immersed in our training program.
