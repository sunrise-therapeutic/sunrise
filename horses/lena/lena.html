Lena

Born: 2002

Gender: Mare

Breed: Morgan X

Height: 14.1 hh

Colour: Chestnut

Lena is a Morgan X chestnut mare, who came to Sunrise in the winter of 2017.
Lena is a very steady and good-natured horse who is wonderful with riders and volunteers alike. She is a particular choice for more independent riders, and is a great all-around horse for our many Sunrise programs.
