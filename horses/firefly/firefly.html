Firefly

Born: 2002

Gender: Mare

Breed: Clydesdale/Thoroughbred

Height: 16 hh

Colour: Chestnut with flaxen mane and tail

Firefly is a Clydesdale/Thoroughbred chestnut mare with a wide blaze.
Firefly arrived in late Spring of 2018, just in time for summer camp. She proved herself to be easygoing, calm, and hardworking. She quickly became a favourite amongst riders and volunteers alike. She has a patient and gentle nature.
Fun Firefly fact: she got her name because she was born late one spring night, and was found resting in the field surrounded by fireflies.
