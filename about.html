Welcome to Sunrise Therapeutic Riding

Our Mission

To develop the full potential of children and adults with special needs through therapy, education, horse riding, recreation, and life‑skills programs.

Why Choose Us?

Our accredited staff and safe, supportive environment allow participants to grow confidence, physical strength, and social connections.

How to Get Involved

Whether you’re looking to volunteer, donate, or enroll in a program, we have options that fit many schedules and interests. Reach out via phone or email for more details.
